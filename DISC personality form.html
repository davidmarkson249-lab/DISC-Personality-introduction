DISC Personality introduction

The DISC model is one of the most popular frameworks used to understand human behavior and personality. It groups behaviors into four main types:

  • D – Dominance: Focused on results, challenges, and direct action.
  • I – Influence: Energetic, persuasive, and motivated by social interaction.
  • S – Steadiness: Patient, calm, and values stability and support.
  • C – Conscientiousness: Careful, detail-oriented, and motivated by accuracy and rules.

The goal of DISC is to help people better understand themselves and others, improve teamwork, communication, and self-awareness.
